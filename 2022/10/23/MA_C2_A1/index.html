Exercise 2.1.1

用导数定义求下列函数的导数:

  1. \(f(x)=\left\{\begin{aligned}&x^2\sin\dfrac{1}{x},\quad x\neq 0\\&0,\quad\quad\quad\quad x=0\end{aligned}\right.\), 求 \(f'(0)\).

  2. \(f(x)=x|x|\), 求 \(f'(0)\).

Solution

\[ f'(0)=\lim_{t\to 0}\dfrac{f(t)-f(0)}{t}=\lim_{t\to 0}\dfrac{t^2\sin\dfrac{1}{t}}{t}=\lim_{t\to 0}t\sin\dfrac{1}{t}=0 \]

\[ f'(0)=\lim_{t\to 0}\dfrac{t|t|-0}{t}=\lim_{t\to 0}|t|=0 \]

Exercise 2.1.2

已知函数 \(f\) 在 \(x_0\) 处可导, 求下列极限:

  1. \(\lim\limits_{h\to 0}\dfrac{f(x_0+h)-f(x_0-h)}{h}\).
  2. \(\lim\limits_{x\to x_0}\dfrac{x_0f(x)-xf(x_0)}{x-x_0}\).

Solution

\[ \lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{f(x_0+h)-f(x_0-h)}{h}=\lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{f(x_0+h)-f(x_0)}{h}+\lim_{h\to 0}\dfrac{f(x_0)-f(x_0-h)}{h}=2f'(x_0) \]

\[ \lim_{x\to x_0}\dfrac{x_0f(x)-xf(x_0)}{x-x_0}=\lim_{x\to x_0}\dfrac{x_0f(x)-x_0f(x_0)}{x-x_0}+\lim_{x\to x_0}\dfrac{x_0f(x_0)-xf(x_0)}{x-x_0}=x_0f'(x_0)-f(x_0) \]

Exercise 2.1.4

求 \(y=\ln x\) 在点 \((\mathrm e,1)\) 的切线方程和法线方程.

Solution

因为 \(y'=\dfrac{1}{x}\), 故切线方程为 \[ y-1=\dfrac{1}{\mathrm{e}}(x-\mathrm{e}) \] 法线方程为 \[ y-1=-\mathrm{e}(x-\mathrm{e}) \]

Exercise 2.2.1

求下列函数的导数:

  1. \(y=\dfrac{1}{1+x+x^2}+\ln 3\).
  2. \(y=\dfrac{2\csc x}{1+x^2}\).
  3. \(y=\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x}}}\).

Solution

  1. 令 \(f(x)=\dfrac{1}{x},g(x)=1+x+x^2\), 则

\[ y'=f(g(x))'=f'(g(x))\cdot g'(x)=-\dfrac{2x+1}{(x^2+x+1)^2} \]

\[ y'=\dfrac{-2\csc x\cot x(1+x^2)-2x\csc x}{(1+x^2)^2}=-2\csc x\dfrac{\cot x(1+x^2)+x}{(1+x^2)^2} \]

\[ y'=\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x}}}}\left(1+\dfrac{1}{2\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x}}}+\dfrac{1}{4\sqrt{x}\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x}}}\right)=\dfrac{1+2\sqrt{x}+4\sqrt{x}\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x}}}{8\sqrt{x}\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x}}\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x+\sqrt{x}}}} \]

Exercise 2.2.3

求下列函数的导数

  1. \(y=\ln(x^2+\cos x)\).
  2. \(y=\left(\arccos\dfrac{1}{x}\right)^2\).
  3. \(y=\ln\sqrt{x\sin x\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x}}\).

Solution

\[ y'=\dfrac{1}{x^2+\cos x}\cdot (x^2+\cos x)'=\dfrac{2x-\sin x}{x^2+\cos x} \]

  1. 因为 \(y\) 在 \((-\infty,0)\) 上和 \((0,+\infty)\) 上分别单调增, 故

\[ y'=2\arccos\dfrac{1}{x}\left(\arccos \dfrac{1}{x}\right)'=2\arccos\dfrac{1}{x}\cdot \dfrac{1}{x\sqrt{x^2-1}}=2\arccos\dfrac{1}{x}\cdot \dfrac{|x|}{x^2\sqrt{x^2-1}} \]

\[ y'=\dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x\sin x\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x}}}(\sqrt{x\sin x\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x}})'=\dfrac{1}{2x\sin x\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x}}(x\sin x\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x})'\\=\dfrac{1}{2x\sin x\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x}}\cdot \left(\dfrac{-\mathrm{e}^x}{2\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x}}+\sqrt{1-\mathrm{e}^x}(x\cos x+\sin x)\right)\\=\dfrac{-\mathrm{e}^x}{4x\sin x(1-\mathrm{e}^x)}+\dfrac{x\cos x+\sin x}{2x\sin x}=\dfrac{1}{4x\sin x(1-\mathrm{e}^x)}+\dfrac{1}{2}\cot x+\dfrac{1}{2x} \]

Exercise 2.2.4

已知 \(y=f\left(\dfrac{3x-2}{3x+2}\right)\),\(f'(x)=\arctan x^2\), 试求 \(\left.\dfrac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}\right|_{x=0}\).

Solution

\[ \left.\dfrac{\mathrm dy}{\mathrm dx}\right|_{x=0}=f'\left(\dfrac{3x-2}{3x+2}\right)\cdot \left.\left(\dfrac{3x-2}{3x+2}\right)'\right|_{x=0}=\arctan \left(\dfrac{3x-2}{3x+2}\right)^2\cdot\left.\dfrac{12}{(3x+2)^2}\right|_{x=0}=\dfrac{3\pi}{4} \]

Exercise 2.2.6

求下列函数的导数 (\(f,g\) 是可导函数):

  1. \(y=f(\sin^2 x)+f(\cos ^2 x)\);
  2. \(y=f(\mathrm{e}^x)\mathrm{e}^{g(x)}\);
  3. \(y=\left\{\begin{aligned}&\dfrac{x}{1+\mathrm{e}^{\frac{1}{x}}},x\neq 0\\&0\ \ \ \ \ \quad \ \ ,x=0\end{aligned}\right.\).

Solution

\[ y'=f'(\sin^2 x)\cdot 2\sin x\cos x- f'(\cos ^2x)\cdot2\sin x\cos x=\sin 2x(f'(\sin ^2x)-f'(\cos ^2 x)) \]

  1. 利用链式法则和导数积的求导法则可得

\[ y'=f(\mathrm e^x)\cdot \mathrm e^{g(x)}\cdot g'(x)+\mathrm{e}^{x+g(x)}\cdot f'(\mathrm e^x) \]

  1. 当 \(x\neq 0\) 时, 有

\[ y'=\dfrac{\frac{1}{\mathrm e^x}\left(1+x\right)+1}{(1+\mathrm e^{\frac{1}{x}})^2} \]

当 \(x=0\) 时, 因为 \[ \lim_{x\to 0^{-}}\dfrac{\frac{x}{1+\mathrm{e}^{1/x}}-0}{x}=\lim_{x\to 0^{-}} \dfrac{1}{1+\mathrm e^{1/x}}=1 \]

\[ \lim_{x\to 0^{+}}\dfrac{\frac{x}{1+\mathrm{e}^{1/x}}-0}{x}=\lim_{x\to 0^{+}} \dfrac{1}{1+\mathrm e^{1/x}}=0 \]

故在 \(x=0\) 处的导数不存在.

综上, 有 \[ y'=\left\{\begin{aligned}\dfrac{\frac{1}{\mathrm{e}^x}(1+x)+1}{(1+\mathrm e^{1/x})^2}, x\neq 0\\\mathrm{undefined}, x=0\end{aligned}\right. \]

Exercise 2.2.9

求下列函数指定阶的导数:

  1. \(f(x)=\mathrm{e}^x\cos x\), 求 \(f^{(4)}(x)\).
  2. \(f(x)=\dfrac{1}{x^2-3x+2}\), 求 \(f^{(n)}(x)\).

Solution

\[ f^{(4)}(x)=\sum_{k=0}^4\binom{4}{k}\mathrm{e}^x(\cos x)^{(k)}=-4\mathrm{e}^x\cos x \]

\[ f^{(n)}(x)=\{[(x-2)(x-1)]^{-1}\}^{(n)}=(-1)^{n}n!\left[\dfrac{1}{(x-2)(x-1)}\right]^{n+1} \]
